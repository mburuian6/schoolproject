General questions

Is account registration required?

Account registration at Test is only done via the admin adding an additional account from within the app.

What are the default admin credentials?

Default admin credentials are:
Username: admin
Password: admin

How do I change default credentials?

When you first login, the app will prompt you to change the login password and set the security question.
The security question is important to recover your password. It is a selected question to which the answer is stored in the app. When you forget your password this will help you recover your account.

I forgot my password. How do I recover my account?

Every account has a security question as a policy for the purpose of recovering the account. Just press the Forgot Password button, give a correct answer and you can reset the account credentials and just go about your business.
Sale

Who can sell items?

Any registered user by the admin can sell a product previously registered on the particular program. The admin is referred to as the primary account and cannot be deleted. The subsequent accounts are referred to as secondary and are controlled by the admin.

I want to sell my items - what are the steps?

The steps involved in this process are really simple. All you need to do is:
  • Login; or have the admin register the account in the case of a secondary account
  • Register a product at either the
    1. Product page
    2. Software page
    This captures the product details including price for automatic computation purposes.
  • Go to the Sale section and choose which products are being sold and the quantity of the product.
  • Commit the sale and voila! The sale is done.

How much do I get from each sale?

Depending on the set price of the product and the quantity of the sale, the app does the calculation, factoring in the discount and gives the total to be paid.

Why sell my items here?

This app aims to make it easier and more accountable for relatively small movie enterprises; especially in terms of book keeping and report features that offer more insights for the business as an independent economic model.

What are the payment options?

This is dependent on the store policies and the agreement between the respective vendor and customer. The app works to support this transaction on the vendor side.
